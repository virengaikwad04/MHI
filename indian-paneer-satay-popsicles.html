Indian Paneer Satay / Popsicles (Recipe from Deepa Kannan)

Nutritional Info:
Energy: 280 kcal, Carbohydrates: 6 g, Fat: 28 g, Fiber: 8 g, Protein: 18 g

Ingredients

Ingredients for Coriander Mint Dip:

  • 1 handful of Coriander Leaves
  • Small quantity of Mint Leaves
  • 1/2 teaspoon Roasted Jeera Powder
  • 1/2 tablespoon Lemon Juice
  • Salt (to taste)
  • 5 tablespoons Hung Curd
  • 1 Green Chilly

Instructions
  1. Combine all the ingredients for the coriander mint dip in a mixer or food processor.
  2. Grind the ingredients into a fine paste, adding water as necessary to achieve the desired consistency.
  3. Adjust the seasoning with salt and lemon juice as needed.

Optional

Grate the idlis with cheese to increase the protein content. Enjoy your low-carb, keto-friendly idlis!
