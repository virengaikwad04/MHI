Successful Stories

Mr SI came in for weight loss post angioplasty. But he had an undiagnosed diabetes. His Hba1c was 8.2 in Apr’22. He was put on a Low Carb Vegetarian diet with fasting regimen. His Hba1c in July’22 was 6.2 without any medications. Weight loss is 23 Kgs in 6 month time. Also lost 10 inches off the hip and 9.5 inches kgs around the waist. Higher energy levels post this lifestyle changes is an added benefit. There are other changes in his cardiovascular health like reversal of LVH (Left ventricular hypertrophy is a thickening of the wall of the heart's main pumping chamber) which was there for many years. The work is on and progress is seen month on month

Mr SI - Bengaluru

Salaj came to me with uncontrolled resistant hypertension (Blood pressure) He was on 4 drugs and still be BP was 160/95 He was put on a non-veg ketogenic diet Within 3 months he was off all 4 drugs He was on Telmisartan 80mg, Chlorthalidone 6.5, Amlodipine 5mg and Bisoprolol 5mg His creatinine was also raised at 1mg/dl At the end of 3 months he was weaned off all 4 drugs with a BP of 120/68 Till today he is well controlled with no meds and on a relaxed low carb diet.

Mr. S Mukerjee – Kolkata
