Low Carb / Keto Tikkis / Patties Recipe

Nutritional Info:
Energy: 280 kcal, Carbohydrates: 6 g, Fat: 28 g, Fiber: 8 g, Protein: 18 g

Ingredients
  • 200gm Paneer
  • 1 tablespoon Coconut Flour
  • 1 teaspoon Psyllium Husk
  • 1 tablespoon Almond Flour
  • Ghee (for frying)
  • Salt
  • Optional: Onions and cheese for topping

Instructions
  1. Put all ingredients in a mixer and grind with minimal water until it forms an almost dry mixture.
  2. Make the mixture into balls and let it sit for some time.
  3. Flatten the balls and fry in a pan or tava with ghee.
  4. If desired, add some onions on top.
  5. Serve with gravy vegetables or your preferred side dish.
  6. Optional: Grate cheese on top of the tikkis/patties to increase the protein content. Enjoy your low-carb, keto-friendly tikkis/patties

Optional

Grate the idlis with cheese to increase the protein content. Enjoy your low-carb, keto-friendly idlis!
