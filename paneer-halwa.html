Paneer Halwa

Nutritional Info:
Energy: 280 kcal, Carbohydrates: 6 g, Fat: 28 g, Fiber: 8 g, Protein: 18 g

Ingredients
  • 1. Paneer (crumbled)
  • 2. Ghee
  • 3. Chopped almonds, pista, cashews, walnuts
  • 4. Crushed cardamom seeds
  • 5. 2 strands of Saffron
  • 6. Stevia or Erythritol (for sweetness)

Instructions
  1. 1. In a kadai, heat ghee. Add chopped almonds, pista, cashews, walnuts, crushed cardamom seeds, and 2 strands of Saffron. Fry until slightly browned.
  2. 2. Add the crumbled paneer and saute until it is well mixed with the nuts and spices. Add additional ghee if needed.
  3. 3. Remove the mixture from heat and add stevia or erythritol for sweetness.
  4. 4. Enjoy hot!
